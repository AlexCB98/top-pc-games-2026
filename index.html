Top PC Games for the Second Half of 2026

The second half of 2026 includes several exciting games for PC players. This selection excludes console-exclusive titles and soulslike games.

July

Halo: Campaign Evolved

A modern remake of the original Halo campaign, featuring improved graphics, updated gameplay and a renewed version of the story that introduced Master Chief.

Other notable releases:

  • Assassin’s Creed Black Flag Resynced
  • Palworld 1.0
  • The Relic: First Guardian

August

Resonance: A Plague Tale Legacy

A story-driven action-adventure game set in the world of A Plague Tale. The game follows a new journey involving danger, mystery and survival.

Other notable releases:

  • Star Wars: Zero Company
  • Beast of Reincarnation
  • Hell Let Loose: Vietnam

September

Control Resonant

A supernatural action game that continues the Control universe. Players can expect unusual environments, powerful abilities and a mysterious story.

Other notable releases:

  • The Blood of Dawnwalker
  • Warhammer 40,000: Dawn of War IV
  • Valheim 1.0
